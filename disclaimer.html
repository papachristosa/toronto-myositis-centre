Medical disclaimer

This website is a medical education resource. The information here is general, and it is not a substitute for advice from your own healthcare team, who know your history and your circumstances.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Education, not medical advice

The content on this site is provided for general education, for both patients and clinicians. Nothing on this site is personal medical advice, a diagnosis, or a treatment recommendation for any individual. It cannot account for your particular history, medications, test results, or circumstances.

2. It does not create a patient relationship

Reading this site or contacting us through it does not create a physician and patient relationship with the Toronto Myositis Centre, its clinicians, or Unity Health Toronto. Care is provided only through a proper clinical referral and appointment.

3. Always check with your own team

Decisions about your care, including starting, stopping, or changing any medicine, should be made with the healthcare professionals who look after you. If something here seems to conflict with what your own team has told you, follow your team and ask them about it.

4. In an emergency

This site is not for emergencies. If you think you are having a medical emergency, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency department. Do not wait for a reply to any message.

5. Accuracy and currency

The content is prepared with care and drawn from recognised clinical sources. Even so, medicine changes, and a page may not reflect the very newest evidence or a recent guideline change. We make no warranty that the content is complete, current, or error free.

6. Links to other sites

Where we link to guidelines, patient organisations, journals, or other resources, those links are provided for convenience. We do not control those sites and are not responsible for their content or their privacy practices.

7. Questions

If you have a question about this disclaimer, contact us at olive.apilas@unityhealth.to. See also our privacy and terms of use pages.
